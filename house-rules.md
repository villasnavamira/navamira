# House Standards & Safety Policy

**NAVAMIRA VILLAS · Santa Susanna · Catalonia · Spain**

*Please read carefully before your arrival. By staying at the villa, you agree to comply with all rules below.*

---

## <img src="icons/rules.png" width="32" valign="middle"> 1. General Rules

The villa is for private tourist use only. All guests must respect public order, observe quiet hours, and behave considerately towards neighbours at all times. All use of the property must comply with this policy and the conditions of the reservation.

---

## <img src="icons/home-safety-100.png" width="32" valign="middle"> 2. Property Security

Each guest is responsible for the security of their own belongings. Guests agree to:

- ✔ Close windows, doors, and blinds when leaving the property
- ✔ Not leave valuables in plain sight
- ✔ Immediately report any malfunction of locks, windows, or access systems

> 📌 The owner is not responsible for lost or stolen items if guests fail to secure the property properly.

---

## <img src="icons/enter-house.png" width="32" valign="middle"> 3. Proper Use of the Property

Guests must treat the villa, furniture, and all equipment with care. Any damage caused must be compensated by the responsible guest.

- ✖ Moving or reorganising furniture without authorisation
- ✖ Using candles or open flames indoors
- ✖ Hanging items from fixtures, shutters, or railings

> 📌 The owner is not liable for injuries resulting from negligence, misuse of equipment, or disregard of safety instructions.

---

## <img src="icons/inspection.png" width="32" valign="middle"> 4. Safety on the Premises

- ✔ Use electrical and gas appliances responsibly
- ✔ Do not leave appliances running unattended
- ✔ Familiarise yourself with emergency switches, fire extinguishers, and the first-aid kit
- ✔ Follow all posted signs and safety notices

**🆘 Emergency numbers**

| Service | Number |
|---|---|
| Emergency (all services) | **112** |
| National Police | **091** |
| Mossos d'Esquadra | **088** |
| Medical emergencies | **061** |
| Fire brigade | **080** |
| Owner (urgent) | **villas@navamira.com** |

---

## <img src="icons/electric-vehicle.png" width="32" valign="middle"> 5. Electricity & Utilities

- ✔ Use air conditioning, heating, and electrical appliances responsibly
- ✔ Switch off lights, AC, and appliances when leaving the villa
- ✔ Do not leave charging devices or appliances running overnight unattended
- ✖ Do not overload electrical sockets or use non-approved adaptors

> 📌 Abusive or excessive consumption of utilities may be charged additionally.

---

## <img src="icons/child-safe-zone.png" width="32" valign="middle"> 6. Children

Children must be supervised at all times, especially near:

- the pool and all water features
- stairs, terraces, and balconies
- elevated areas and railings

Parents and guardians are fully responsible for incidents involving minors due to lack of supervision or disregard of safety rules.

---

## <img src="icons/pharma.png" width="32" valign="middle"> 7. Allergies & Medical Conditions

The villa may contain insects, pollen, dust, humidity, and common cleaning chemicals typical for the region. Guests are responsible for managing their own medical conditions and medications.

> 📌 The owner is not liable for allergic reactions unless informed in advance.

---

## <img src="icons/good-quality.png" width="32" valign="middle"> 8. Acceptance of Risks

By staying at the villa, guests accept inherent risks including:

- an unattended pool (no lifeguard present)
- potential slips or falls
- weather conditions (wind, heat, rain)
- insects or local wildlife

Guests remain responsible for their personal safety. The owner is not liable for injuries resulting from negligent, reckless, or improper use of the property or its facilities.

---

## <img src="icons/no-smoking.png" width="32" valign="middle"> 9. No Smoking

🚭 Smoking is strictly prohibited inside the villa, including:

- cigarettes, cigars, and pipes
- electronic cigarettes and vapes
- any prohibited substances

- ✔ Smoking is permitted **outdoors only**
- ✔ Cigarette butts must be disposed of in provided ashtrays — never on the ground, especially during fire-risk periods

> ⚠️ Violations will result in a specialist cleaning charge (minimum €300) and may lead to termination of stay without refund.

---

## <img src="icons/pets.png" width="32" valign="middle"> 10. Pets

Pets are permitted **only with prior written authorisation**. If approved:

- ✔ The pet must be supervised at all times
- ✔ Guests must clean up after their pet throughout the property
- ✔ Pets are not permitted in pool areas or on interior soft furnishings
- ✔ Guests are responsible for any damage or extraordinary cleaning caused by their pet

> ⚠️ Undeclared pets may result in additional charges and/or eviction.

---

## <img src="icons/people.png" width="32" valign="middle"> 11. Occupancy & Visitors

The maximum occupancy permitted by the HUT licence is **10 persons at any time**, including both registered guests and any day visitors.

- ✔ Overnight stays are reserved exclusively for guests registered in the booking
- ✔ Day visitors are permitted provided the **total number of persons on the property does not exceed 10** at any moment
- ✔ All visitors must leave before quiet hours begin (21:00 outdoors / 22:00 indoors)

> ⚠️ **Additional person charge:** Because our nightly rate is based on the number of guests and we cannot verify the duration of any visit, any person present on the property — regardless of the length of their stay — is subject to a daily rate of **€20 per person per day.**

> 🚫 Exceeding the maximum of 10 persons constitutes a serious breach and may result in **immediate termination of the stay without refund.**

---

## <img src="icons/party.png" width="32" valign="middle"> 12. Parties & Events

Parties, large gatherings, and events exceeding the number of registered guests are strictly prohibited.

- ✖ No events or celebrations with external attendees
- ✖ No amplified music or DJ equipment at any time
- ✖ No marquees, temporary structures, or external catering without written authorisation

> 🚫 Unauthorised parties may result in immediate termination of stay without refund and a minimum charge of **€750.**

---

## <img src="icons/bullet-camera.png" width="32" valign="middle"> 13. Security Cameras

The villa is equipped with an **outdoor-only** security camera system. All cameras are clearly signposted.

**📍 Camera locations & schedule**

| Location | Operation |
|---|---|
| Main entrance, exterior gate & parking | Continuous recording — 24 h / 7 days |
| Perimeter access point towards pool area | Motion-activated · **00:00 – 07:00 only** · completely inactive outside these hours |

The pool-area camera is aimed at the **access point only** — not at the water, pool deck, or guest leisure areas.

**Cameras do NOT:**
- ✖ Monitor guest behaviour or leisure activities
- ✖ Record pool use or sunbathing areas
- ✖ Identify individuals or count guests
- ✖ Observe personal routines or conduct

Camera schedules are fixed and **are not modified on request.**

Footage from the entrance camera is retained for a **maximum of 30 days** and then permanently deleted, unless required by law enforcement authorities.

> 🚫 Covering, disconnecting, redirecting, or tampering with any camera is strictly prohibited and will result in a **€200 inspection/reset charge per camera**, notification to the booking platform, and may lead to immediate termination of stay without refund.

---

## <img src="icons/noise.png" width="32" valign="middle"> 14. Quiet Hours & Noise

| Zone | Quiet hours |
|---|---|
| 🏠 Indoors | **22:00 – 09:00** |
| 🌳 Outdoors (terrace, garden, pool) | **21:00 – 09:00** |

During quiet hours, guests must:
- ✔ Avoid loud conversations outdoors
- ✔ Avoid gatherings on terraces, garden, or pool areas
- ✔ Refrain from pool use

**Music and amplified sound outdoors is not permitted at any time of day.**

> ⚠️ Documented noise complaints may result in termination of the stay without refund.

---

## <img src="icons/lap-pool.png" width="32" valign="middle"> 15. Pool & Water Areas

**Pool hours: 08:00 – 22:00.** No pool use outside these hours.

- ✔ Children must be supervised by a responsible adult at all times — no exceptions
- ✔ Shower before entering the pool
- ✖ Jumping, diving, or acrobatics from the pool edge, terraces, or any elevated structure
- ✖ Glass, sharp objects, or food in the pool water
- ✖ Pool use under the influence of alcohol or substances
- ✖ Loud games, shouting, or disruptive behaviour near the pool

> 📌 No lifeguard is present. Use of the pool is entirely at the guest's own risk.

---

## <img src="icons/balcony.png" width="32" valign="middle"> 16. Terraces & Balconies

- ✖ Sitting, standing, or leaning on railings or parapet walls
- ✖ Throwing objects from elevated areas
- ✖ Accessing rooftops, coverings, or areas not designated for guest use

These restrictions exist for your safety. The owner bears no responsibility for injuries resulting from disregard of these rules.

---

## <img src="icons/registration.png" width="32" valign="middle"> 17. Guest Registration & Data

In accordance with Spanish law (Real Decreto 933/2021), **all guests over 14 years of age** must provide full identity details before or at check-in. This information is reported to the relevant authorities (Mossos d'Esquadra / National Police) within 24 hours of arrival.

Personal data is processed solely for:
- ✔ Legally mandated reporting to authorities
- ✔ Calculation of tourist taxes
- ✔ Verifying authorised access to the property
- ✔ Resolving contractual issues related to occupancy

Personal data is **never** used for profiling, marketing, or behaviour monitoring. Security camera footage is retained for a maximum of **30 days.**

Guests may exercise their GDPR rights by contacting: **villas@navamira.com**
